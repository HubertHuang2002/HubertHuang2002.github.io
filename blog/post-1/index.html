Introduction to NTU BA

By Hubert Huang 01 July 2024

In the following article, we use “NTU BA” to refer to the department of Business Administration at National Taiwan University.

A Brief Introduction to NTU BA

NTU BA is affiliated with the College of Management at National Taiwan University. Our curriculum includes foundational business courses such as calculus, statistics, economics, accounting, and financial management. We also offer unique courses compared to other departments in the College of Management, such as marketing management, strategic management, and management science model. Most notably, we offer supply chain courses** that are less common in other universities’ BA departments, such as optimization methods, supply chain management, and logistics management.

Skills development

The required credits for core courses in NTU BA are fewer compared to most other departments, allowing us enough time to develop various specialties. Furthermore, our department offers four applied modules for study, including:

  • Marketing
  • Data Analytics
  • Human Resources
  • Technology Management

However, many students also focus on fields such as computer sciences, industrial engineering, mathematics, or other areas.

Daily life in NTU BA

Because our department offers a more flexible curriculum, many seniors start businesses during their student years. There are also numerous students who intern in various industries, and many participate in the department’s mentorship program. Therefore, NTU BA is very suitable for students who want to explore their future paths.

Summary

Freedom, diversity, and exploration are the feelings I’ve experienced in NTU BA. I hope this article can help satisfy the curiosity of those interested in NTU BA.
