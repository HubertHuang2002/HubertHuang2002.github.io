2024 TEDxNational Taiwan University Annual Exhibition

By Hubert Huang 10 December 2024

Brief Introduction

In August 2024, I joined the TEDxNational Taiwan University organizing team as the Director of Public Relations.

Our annual exhibition, the first large-scale event we have hosted, took place from December 2 to December 6, 2024, at National Taiwan University.

About the Annual Exhibition

The annual exhibition is the most significant event of TEDxNational Taiwan University during the first semester. Its main goal is to present the concepts and issues we aim to explore this year through an engaging exhibition.

The theme of this year’s annual exhibition “Life Playlist” seeks to integrate the concept of a playlist with the annual theme “Pause”. It aims to guide attendees in stepping away from their daily routines to reflect on and reconsider the issues in their lives from a new perspective.

My Role

I led the Public Relations team in establishing business collaborations with companies.

Since our annual exhibition was free to attend, we lacked additional revenue to support its organization. Therefore, the Public Relations team worked closely with various companies to secure sufficient funding for the event.

Thanks to the generous support from our partners, we obtained over NT$100,000 in sponsorships, enabling the successful execution of the exhibition, which attracted over 1,500 visitors.

Reflections

First and foremost, I am deeply grateful to all the companies that supported our nonprofit event. Without their help, it would have been challenging to organize this exhibition.

Additionally, I want to thank our entire organizing team. Each member put their utmost effort into their respective responsibilities, and it was our teamwork that made this event such a success.

Lastly, I want to thank every attendee who visited the exhibition. Your participation gave us tremendous support, and many expressed their gratitude and encouragement to the team, which serves as a powerful motivation for us to keep striving forward.
