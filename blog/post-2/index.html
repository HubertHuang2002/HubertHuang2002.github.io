Introduction to the BA Summer Camp

By Hubert Huang 05 August 2024

Introduction to the BA Summer Camp

“The BA Summer Camp” stands for “the Business Administration Summer Camp”, and it is specifically organized for Taiwanese high school students to learn about business administration and related fields.

The Camp’s Curriculum and Content

As mentioned earlier, our primary goal is to introduce high school students to the field of business administration. Therefore, the curriculum focuses on the five key areas of business administration:

  • Production
  • Marketing
  • Human Resources
  • Research and Development
  • Finance

In terms of professional courses, we invite industry experts with practical experience to teach the specialized classes. Additionally, we design business-related games ourselves to engage students and introduce them to business administration concepts in an interactive way.

The Reasons I Volunteer as a Teacher

The first reason is gratitude and contentment. I feel that I have received a lot of help from many people throughout my educational journey, including teachers, classmates, mentors, and parents. Therefore, I hope to give back to students within my capacity.

Secondly, I am a former participant of this camp. The camp not only introduced me to the field of business administration but also allowed me to make many good friends from different schools. Therefore, I believe this camp is very worthwhile and should continue to be supported and held.

My Role at the Camp

I am responsible for leading a particular team composed of high school students from various cities across Taiwan. In addition to leading these students through the courses, I also need to foster team spirit and address various questions about university and business administration.

Due to the uniqueness of my interests and academic background, “Finding Your Own Position” resonates deeply with me. As a result, I am also a speaker on this topic, responsible for sharing how to discover one’s own interests and role, with the goal of inspiring students who feel uncertain about their future.

Summary

I am very pleased to have a group of like-minded university students volunteering with me to support the organization of this camp. I highly recommend that friends who share similar values join in supporting this camp and encourage high school students to participate in similar activities to explore their interests.
